THE WALK — KML → GeoJSON Converter

Drop a KML/KMZ from Google Earth/My Maps. We’ll convert to GeoJSON, set defaults, and you can edit properties before saving to config/zones.geojson.

Tips: Ensure each Feature is a Point with properties: id, radius_m, fadeDistance (optional), maxVolume (optional), and audioLayers (array of { id, file, loop, volume }).
